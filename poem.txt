Two_roads_diverged_in_a_yellow_wood,_
And_sorry_I_could_not_travel_both_
And_be_ore_traveler,_long_I_stood
And_looked_down_one_as_far_as_I_could_
to_where_it_bent_in_the_undergrowth;

then_took_the_other,_as_just_as_fair,_
And_having_perhaps_the_better_claim,
Because_it_was_grassy_and_wanted_wear;_
though_as_for_that_the_passing_there_
Had_worn_them_really_about_the_same,

And_both_that_morning_equally_lay_
In_leaves_no_step_had_trodden_black._
Oh,_I_kept_the_first_for_another_day!_
Yet_knowing_how_way_leads_on_to_way,_
I_doubted_if_I_should_ever_come_back.
